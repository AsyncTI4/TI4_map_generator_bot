# Privacy Policy

This policy has been last updated on June 12, 2026.

Please keep in mind that the [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) should apply by the very nature of the usage of **AsyncTI4** via Discord.

**AsyncTI4** only stores the information necessary to facilitate a game of TI4, and the information that is strictly necessary for its operations. This means:
* When using most of its application commands, **AsyncTI4** stores the information you provide that is relevant to the TI4 game state and your usage of the application.;
* For the purposes of debugging, if the application encounters an unexpected/unhandled error, it will send the full data on the interaction that caused it to the error log. You can read on what will be included there in the Discord Developer Portal documentation. 

While none of this data is publicly-accessible, you still shouldn't store any sensitive data.
