## Fog of War (FoW)

For those who thrive on uncertainty and discovery.
Fog of War introduces a sense of realism — you see only what’s around you. Beyond that lies the unknown.

__**Features:**__
> • Only systems adjacent to those you control are visible.
> • Opponents’ play areas and factions are concealed — you won’t know:
>    – who they are
>    – which technologies they’ve mastered
>    – what strategy cards they hold
>    – or even who truly leads the score

And that’s only the beginning.
FoW is renowned for strange, unpredictable modes — the kind that twist your mind and rewrite the rules you thought you knew.
