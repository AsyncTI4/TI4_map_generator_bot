This channel is for the GM to set up and manage the game.

__**Getting Started:**__
> `/game setup` – Set VP amount, game name, auto-ping time, and options
> `/fow fow_options` – Adjust Fog of War settings
> `/map` – Import, create, or modify the map
> `/player setup` – Assign player factions and colors
> `/game set_order` – Set player turn order

__**Other Useful Commands:**__
> `/game info` – View current game settings
> `/fow show_game_as` – See the map from a player's perspective
> `endofround1` Notes here – Save round summary notes as GM
